Nitrile

Nitrile Gloves

Nitrile gloves are a popular choice for workers in various industries due to their superior strength, chemical resistance, and flexibility. Made from synthetic rubber, nitrile gloves offer excellent protection against oils, solvents, and chemicals, making them ideal for the healthcare, automotive, and chemical industries. They are also latex-free, reducing the risk of allergic reactions, and provide a snug fit with tactile sensitivity, ensuring comfort and precision for tasks requiring dexterity.

Leather

Leather Gloves

Leather gloves are known for their durability, comfort, and protection, making them a staple in heavy-duty work environments. Crafted from cowhide, pigskin, or goatskin, these gloves offer exceptional abrasion resistance and protection from cuts, punctures, and harsh elements. Leather gloves are commonly used in industries such as construction, welding, and farming, providing a sturdy barrier against mechanical hazards while allowing for a comfortable, breathable fit for prolonged wear.

Kevlar

Kevlar Gloves

Kevlar gloves are made from a high-strength, heat-resistant synthetic fiber that offers superior protection against cuts, abrasions, and high temperatures. Known for their lightweight and flexible nature, Kevlar gloves provide excellent tactile sensitivity without compromising safety. These gloves are often used in industries such as manufacturing, assembly, and handling sharp materials, where workers need protection from mechanical risks while maintaining precision and dexterity.
